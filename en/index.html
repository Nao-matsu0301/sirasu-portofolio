Who is Osame Sirasu？

profile

In the winter of my sophomore year of high school, my parents' finances became difficult due to the COVID-19 disaster, and I decided to make my living without being too much of a burden on my parents. In 2018, I created a national poster for a space post collaboration with JAXA, and in the same year, I made a performance debut at the Meiji Shrine in Tokyo, switching from illustrator to artist.

In addition to live performances, I create art on sliding doors, shutters, pillars, etc. In 2018, I created a national poster in collaboration with JAXA and Space Post, and in 2018, I made a performance debut at Meiji Shrine in Tokyo, switching from an illustrator to an artist.
